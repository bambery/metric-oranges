in order to obtain this data, inside the jhu folder, clone down the desired folder:

`git clone --no-checkout git@github.com:CSSEGISandData/COVID-19.git`
`cd COVID-19`
`git sparse-checkout init --cone`
`git checkout @`
`git sparse-checkout set csse_covid_19_data/csse_covid_19_daily_reports`

weekly_us/
This folder and its contents are generated by process_inputs/jhu.py. The contents of the above dir are taken and processed to remove non US FIPS entries, sum, and then output the weekly deaths per FIPS.
